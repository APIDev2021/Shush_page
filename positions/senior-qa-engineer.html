Senior QA Engineer

Remote
Full - time

Job Description

We are seeking an experienced and talented Senior QA Engineer to join our team at Quantango. As a Senior QA Engineer, you will play a key role in designing and implementing testing strategies, ensuring seamless integration of quality assurance processes with development workflows, and optimizing the overall quality and performance of our software products.

Responsibilities

  • Collaborate with the team to plan testing activities.
  • Test and verify web applications and APIs.
  • Identify and escalate release bugs and production incidents.
  • Support the development team by providing business- and technical-related information.
  • Determine and estimate the testing scope at different stages of the SDLC.
  • Develop and maintain detailed, comprehensive, and well-structured test cases, and manage them within test suites.
  • Collect necessary information/documents from various sources and evaluate it for testing purposes, providing valuable feedback to Product Owners.
  • Actively participate in the team’s daily activities and Scrum ceremonies.

Requirements

  • A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering.
  • 4+ years of experience in software testing.
  • Deep understanding of the software development life cycle (SDLC).
  • Strong knowledge of software testing principles.
  • Experience with API testing using tools like Postman or similar.
  • Proven ability to analyze requirements, design tests, execute test cases, and report results.
  • Proficiency with bug tracking systems, including the creation and verification of bug reports.
  • Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies.
  • Knowledge of the operations of both relational and non-relational databases.
  • Experience in constructing SQL queries.
  • Experience using logging and performance tools such as Kibana, Grafana, etc.
  • Capable of independently managing testing processes with complete responsibility from beginning to end.
  • Skilled at adjusting to dynamic environments and varying requirements, with an ability to reprioritize tasks as necessary.
  • Possesses excellent analytical abilities to thoroughly investigate issues and identify underlying causes.
  • Proficient in handling multiple tasks simultaneously and swiftly transitioning between them while maintaining focus and efficiency.

Benefits

  • Competitive and attractive compensation
  • Hybrid work schedule
  • Proper rest time of 24 annual leave days
  • Challenging and unique tasks in the FinTech field
  • Funding for gym memberships, fostering a healthy work-life balance
